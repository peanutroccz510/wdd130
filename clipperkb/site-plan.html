Introduction to the Clipper Knowledge Base Site

This website is designed to be an efficient tool for our Clipper Team. This will serve as a knowledge base with step-by-steps, tutorials and detailed information that will help our Team as they service our customers. With this site, our Team Members can feel at ease with knowing where to go, how to access their needs quickly and gather the information needed as talk to our customers.
