HOW TO RIDE ON BUS

1. Research your start and end point.

2. Be sure to check the Bus you go on is the correct one.

3. Show up at the Bus Stop about 5 min. before it arrives.

4. Pay your fare and put money in the cashbox. OR if you have a transit card, tag your card to the reader to pay.

4. Find a seat.

5. Watch the sign on top and listen to announcements for approaching stops

6. Once you hear your stop, tug on the wire in the window to signal a "Stop"

7. Once the bus stops at your exit, you may exit the bus.
