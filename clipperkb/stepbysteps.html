Manual Balance Transfer

1. Open the Transit Card in CRM.

2. Click "New Incident"

3. In the Description Box, type in the texter "transbt"

Be sure to fill out all the information in the texter.

4. In the Resolution Box, type in "date/time stamp and initials with a note of what was advised to the patron and what action was taken."
